Salt Lake Valley's Best Distilleries - Summer 2025

#5 Salt Flats Distillery

Clean vodka and underrated bourbon with a cool racing theme. Solid choice for classic spirits.

#4 Outlaw Distillery

Fun, Western-themed spot with sweet flavored whiskeys. Their peanut butter whiskey is a crowd-pleaser.

#3 Waterpocket Distillery

Unique gin and agave spirits steal the show. Modern space with creative, high-quality offerings.

#2 Dented Brick Distillery

Locally sourced grains shine in their rich rum and peppery rye. Great rustic vibe and informative tours.

#1 Sugar House Distillery

Smooth, small-batch spirits with a standout bourbon. Cozy tasting room and friendly staff make it a must-visit.
