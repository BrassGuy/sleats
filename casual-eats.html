Salt Lake Valley's Best Casual Eats - Summer 2025

#5 Ruth's Diner

A historic diner with mile-high biscuits, legendary burgers, and epic patio views. The Mormon Toast (French toast with homemade berry syrup) is a brunch staple. Old-school charm meets hearty comfort food.

#4 Curry Fried Chicken

Korean-style fried chicken meets Indian spices in this unassuming spot. The half-and-half combo (soy garlic + spicy curry) is iconic, and the crispy-onion-loaded fries are addictive. Sweet, spicy, and utterly unique.

#3 Tacos Don Rafa

A no-frills taco truck turned local legend, serving authentic Mexican street tacos (al pastor, carne asada) with homemade salsas. The quesadillas doradas are next-level. Cash only, worth every penny.

#2 Laziz Kitchen

Lebanese street food done right, with fluffy garlicky toum, crispy falafel, and the best hummus in town. The shawarma plate is a crowd-pleaser, and the baklava milkshake is a game-changer. Fast, fresh, and full of flavor.
