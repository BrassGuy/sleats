About Us

We create seasonally curated experiences that connect Salt Lake Valley locals and visitors to the best of our region's vibrant food and drink scene. From craft brews and small-batch spirits to local wines and farm-to-table dining—whether fine, casual, or food truck fare—we highlight the people and flavors that make Utah unique. Through pop-ups, tastings, and culinary adventures, we celebrate the community behind every bite and sip.
