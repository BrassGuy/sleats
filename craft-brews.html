Salt Lake Valley's Best Breweries - Summer 2025

#5 Shades Brewing

Shades is all about sours, fruited beers, and unique flavorsâ€”think Apricot Sour and Peanut Butter Porter. If you're tired of traditional styles, this place is a fun detour.

#4 Proper Brewing Co.

A staple in SLC's beer scene, Proper offers bold flavors across the board. Their Proper Burger pairs perfectly with their Proper Beer (a solid English-style bitter). The Double IPA is a hop-lover's dream.

#3 Kiitos Brewing

Kiitos is famous for its stouts and barrel-aged beers, but their West Coast IPA is also a winner. The taproom is spacious, dog-friendly, and often hosts live music.

#2 TF Brewing

Known for its German-inspired brews, TF Brewing excels in clean, well-crafted lagers and ales. The Rauchbier (smoked beer) is a standout, and their Pilsner is one of the best in Utah.

#1 Fisher Brewing Company

A historic brewery revived with a modern twist, Fisher Brewing offers a fantastic mix of classic and experimental beers. Their Helles Lager is crisp and refreshing, while their IPA selection balances bitterness and fruitiness perfectly.
