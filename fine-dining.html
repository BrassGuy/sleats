Salt Lake Valley's Finest Dining - Summer 2025

#5 Franck's (Holladay)

A hidden gem offering French-Mediterranean fare in an unassuming strip mall. The duck confit, escargot, and souffl√©s are worth the pilgrimage. Cozy, authentic, and utterly delicious.

#4 Harbor Seafood & Steak (Downtown)

SLC's go-to for seafood and prime cuts, with an old-school, clubby vibe. The oysters, lobster bisque, and tableside Caesar salad are classics. Perfect for power lunches or indulgent dinners.

#3 Urban Hill (Downtown)

A modern, high-energy spot with shareable plates (think dry-aged ribeye, smoked trout dip) and a chic rooftop bar. The seasonal menu and bold flavors cater to foodies who love innovation.

#2 Log Haven (Millcreek Canyon)

A mountain retreat with award-winning New American cuisine, set in a historic log mansion. Known for dishes like elk tenderloin and molten chocolate cake, paired with a stellar wine list. Bonus: The waterfall views make it uniquely Utah.

#1 Valter's Osteria (Downtown)

The pinnacle of Italian fine dining in SLC, Valter's offers an intimate, white-tablecloth experience with handmade pastas, impeccable wine pairings, and legendary service (helmed by Valter himself). Must-try: The signature gnocchi al Gorgonzola. Ideal for a special occasion or romantic evening.
