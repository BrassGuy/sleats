Salt Lake Valley's Best Food Trucks - Summer 2025

#3 Waffle Love

Sweet tooth salvation with liege waffles (dense, caramelized perfection). The "Love Berry" (whipped cream, berries, syrup) and savory bacon-cheddar waffle split the difference between breakfast and dessert. Pro Tip: Go late-night—they're open till 2am on weekends.

#2 Smokin Bones BBQ

Texas-style BBQ with Utah flair—think melt-in-your-mouth brisket, sticky-sweet pork ribs, and loaded baked potatoes. Reddit Raves: "Best brisket in the state, truck or brick-and-mortar." Often at breweries.

#1 El Morelense

The undisputed birria king of SLC food trucks, serving fall-off-the-bone stewed goat (birria de chivo) and legendary consomé-dipped quesatacos. Reddit's Verdict: Worth the 45-minute wait—life-changing tacos. Cash only.
