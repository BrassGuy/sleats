Salt Lake Valley's Best Wine Destinations - Summer 2025

#5 Under Current Bar & Kitchen

Rounding out the list, this underground gem specializes in boutique and sustainable wines, served in a moody, speakeasy-like setting. The small bites (like whipped feta and lamb meatballs) are divine. A must for a date night or deep wine dive.

#4 Bar à Vin

A French-inspired wine bar with a relaxed, European feel. Their selection leans toward Old World classics (Burgundy, Bordeaux, Rhône), and the bartenders excel at guiding guests. Perfect for Francophiles and traditionalists.

#3 Casot

A natural wine lover's paradise, Casot offers funky, unfiltered, and organic wines in a cozy, eclectic space. The small plates (like Basque-style tapas) are a perfect match. Best for adventurous drinkers seeking low-intervention wines.

#2 BTG Wine Bar

BTG ("By The Glass") is a sommelier-driven hotspot with a constantly evolving wine list and a chic, intimate vibe. Their expert pairings (like charcuterie and artisan cheeses) elevate the experience. Ideal for high-end, curated tastings.

#1 The Tasting Room

A sleek, modern wine bar offering an extensive selection of global wines with a focus on education. Their rotating flights and knowledgeable staff make it perfect for both novices and connoisseurs. Great for exploring rare finds in a stylish setting.
